Paint Correction
Paint & Coat
Request a Quote

A professional machine polishing process that physically removes swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and oxidation — restoring your paint to a true mirror-like finish. This is the recommended service before ceramic coating.

What's Included
  • Full decontamination wash and clay bar treatment
  • Paint depth and gloss measurement (before & after)
  • Single or multi-stage machine polishing
  • Removal of swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation
  • Panel wipe-down and inspection under lighting
  • Finishing polish for maximum gloss and clarity
  • Paint sealant applied as final protection
Pricing varies by vehicle

Paint correction is assessed on a case-by-case basis. Pricing depends on your vehicle's paint condition, size, and the level of correction required — so we build every quote around your specific situation.

  • Vehicle size and panel count
  • Severity of swirls, scratches, and oxidation
  • Number of correction stages needed
  • Current paint condition and thickness

Ceramic Coating
Paint & Coat
Request a Quote

The ultimate long-term investment in your vehicle's finish. A nano-ceramic layer bonds to your paint and provides years of protection against UV, contaminants, bird droppings, and water spots — while delivering an unmatched, deep gloss.

What's Included
  • Full decontamination wash and clay bar treatment
  • Paint correction to ensure a flawless base
  • IPA panel wipe to remove all polish residue
  • Professional-grade ceramic coating applied by hand
  • Curing time and final inspection under lighting
  • Aftercare guide and maintenance tips provided
  • 2–5 year protection warranty depending on package
Pricing varies by vehicle

Ceramic coating pricing is built around your vehicle's needs — every job includes paint correction as a base, but the total cost depends on your paint's current condition, vehicle size, and the coating package you choose.

  • Vehicle size and panel layout
  • Current paint condition (determines correction level)
  • Coating package selected (1-year to 5-year protection)
  • Additional surfaces (glass, wheels, trim)
